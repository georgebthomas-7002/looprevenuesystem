Workshops and Exercises

Learn by doing. Build together.

Participate in hands-on workshops and team exercises designed to help you internalize Loop principles and align your team around a shared system.

Team Workshops

Collaborative sessions that bring your team together to build shared understanding and alignment.

Loop Fundamentals Workshop

Duration: 2 hours | Team size: 5-15 people

Get everyone on the same page about what the Loop is, why it matters, and how it will transform your revenue operations.

What you'll do:

  • Map your current customer journey as a team
  • Identify gaps and disconnects in your process
  • Design your ideal Loop flow collaboratively
  • Create a shared vision for implementation

Customer Journey Mapping Workshop

Duration: 3 hours | Team size: 6-20 people

Build a complete view of how customers experience your business from first touch through advocacy.

What you'll do:

  • Document every customer touchpoint
  • Identify moments that matter most
  • Spot friction points and opportunities
  • Design improvements across all four Loops

Cross-Loop Integration Workshop

Duration: 2.5 hours | Team size: 8-25 people

Align marketing, sales, service, and ops teams around seamless handoffs and shared goals.

What you'll do:

  • Map current handoff points between teams
  • Identify information gaps and delays
  • Design ideal transition processes
  • Create accountability for Loop continuity

Metrics and Governance Workshop

Duration: 2 hours | Team size: 4-12 people

Define the metrics that matter and establish the governance structure to keep your Loops healthy.

What you'll do:

  • Identify your critical revenue metrics
  • Design your metrics dashboard
  • Establish data governance rules
  • Create a continuous improvement cadence

Certification Programs

Structured learning paths that build deep expertise in the Loop Revenue System.

Loop Practitioner Certification

For individual contributors who implement and manage day-to-day Loop operations.

Includes:

  • 10 hours of guided learning
  • Hands-on implementation exercises
  • Real-world scenario practice
  • Final certification assessment

Loop Strategist Certification

For leaders and RevOps professionals who design and optimize revenue systems.

Includes:

  • 15 hours of advanced training
  • Strategic planning workshops
  • Optimization case studies
  • Peer learning community access
