Service Loop

How Express, Tailor, Amplify, and Evolve apply to onboarding, support, retention, and expansion

The Service Loop in Action

Service teams use the Loop Revenue System to deliver consistent, exceptional customer experiences that drive retention and growth. Every customer interaction, every support ticket, and every expansion opportunity follows the same framework.

Express

Define your service standards, response templates, and customer success playbooks. Create knowledge base content and AI-assisted support tools that maintain quality.

Tailor

Segment customers by health score, usage patterns, and lifecycle stage. Personalize onboarding, support, and expansion strategies based on customer data.

Amplify

Transform best practices into scalable assets: onboarding checklists, help articles, video tutorials, expansion playbooks, and QBR templates.

Evolve

Track CSAT, NPS, retention rates, and expansion revenue. Use feedback loops and support analytics to continuously improve the customer experience.

Service in HubSpot Service Hub

The Service Loop is built natively into HubSpot Service Hub, giving you a complete system for managing customer success:

  • Customer Success Playbooks and Standard Operating Procedures
  • Customer Segmentation with Health Scores and Properties
  • Ticket Management and Support Automation
  • Knowledge Base and Self-Service Resources
  • Customer Feedback and NPS Tracking
