Marketing Loop

How Express, Tailor, Amplify, and Evolve apply to demand gen, content, brand, and campaigns

The Marketing Loop in Action

Marketing teams use the Loop Revenue System to create consistent, data-driven campaigns that align with sales and service. Every piece of content, every campaign, and every interaction follows the same four-stage framework.

Express

Define your brand voice, positioning, and messaging framework. Create content guidelines and AI prompts that ensure consistency across all marketing channels.

Tailor

Segment audiences based on ICP fit, lifecycle stage, and engagement data. Personalize content and campaigns using HubSpot properties and smart content.

Amplify

Transform core ideas into full campaign assets: blog posts, social content, email sequences, landing pages, and sales enablement materials.

Evolve

Track campaign performance, content engagement, and pipeline contribution. Use data to refine messaging, optimize channels, and improve conversion rates.

Marketing in HubSpot Marketing Hub

The Marketing Loop is built natively into HubSpot Marketing Hub, giving you a complete system for managing your marketing operations:

  • Brand Voice & Content Strategy in Content Hub
  • Audience Segmentation with Lists and Workflows
  • Campaign Management with Email, Forms, and Landing Pages
  • Performance Tracking with Attribution and Analytics
  • AI-Powered Content Creation with Brand Guidelines
