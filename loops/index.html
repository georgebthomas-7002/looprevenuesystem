The Four Stages of Every Loop

Each team goes through these same four stages, creating alignment across your entire organization.

1. Express

Teach your organization and your AI tools how your brand sounds, what you stand for, and who you serve.

2. Tailor

Use clean data and real context so every contact gets the next best step at the right time.

3. Amplify

Take one strong idea and turn it into a full set of assets for every team to use.

4. Evolve

Measure what matters across the full journey and improve based on actual impact.
