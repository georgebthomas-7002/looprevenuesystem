Sales Loop

How Express, Tailor, Amplify, and Evolve apply to prospecting, enablement, deal management, and handoffs

The Sales Loop in Action

Sales teams use the Loop Revenue System to create consistent, repeatable processes that work in harmony with marketing and service. Every prospect interaction, every deal, and every handoff follows the same framework.

Express

Define your sales playbook, talk tracks, and value propositions. Create enablement materials and AI-assisted templates that maintain your voice in every conversation.

Tailor

Qualify leads based on ICP fit and readiness signals. Personalize outreach and proposals using contact properties, company data, and engagement history.

Amplify

Turn winning strategies into reusable assets: email templates, call scripts, demo flows, proposal templates, and objection handling guides.

Evolve

Track deal velocity, win rates, and rep performance. Use conversation intelligence and deal analysis to refine your approach continuously.

Sales in HubSpot Sales Hub

The Sales Loop is built natively into HubSpot Sales Hub, giving you a complete system for managing your sales operations:

  • Sales Playbooks and Talk Tracks in the CRM
  • Lead Scoring and ICP Qualification with Properties
  • Deal Pipeline Management with Custom Stages
  • Email Templates and Sequences for Consistency
  • Sales Analytics and Forecasting
